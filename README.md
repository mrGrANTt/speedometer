# Speedometer
A simple mod that adds a speedometer to your GUI (‼️requaried <a href="https://modrinth.com/mod/cloth-config">cloth-config</a>‼️)

Mod show your moving speed, yaw and pitch on left top of screen. It looks at the distance traveled in a short period of time (you can manage it).
